By refining the mechanical constraints and expanding the search space to include predicted stable phases (discovery vs. screening), the paper moves from a standard screening exercise to a high-impact contribution in materials informatics. The ROI of effort is high because the core data processing is complete; the remaining work is primarily analytical refinement and validation, which will significantly strengthen the manuscript's credibility for a top-tier materials journal.
