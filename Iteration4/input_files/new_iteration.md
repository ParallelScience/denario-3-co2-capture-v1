- Decision: ITERATE
- Justification: The current project has successfully established a high-quality thermodynamic screening pipeline, but the proposed improvements address the "Publishability Delta" required to transition from a computational exercise to a high-impact materials science paper. Specifically, the transition from a static weighted score to a Pareto-front analysis (addressing the "black box" ranking) and the inclusion of kinetic/structural constraints (volume expansion and diffusion bottlenecks) are essential for peer-review credibility. The "isostructural substitution" strategy for the top-performing mixed-alkaline-earth oxides provides a clear, novel design rule that shifts the paper from mere "screening" to "materials design," significantly increasing its scientific impact. The ROI is high because the infrastructure for the analysis is already built; these refinements leverage existing data to provide deeper, more actionable physical insights without requiring a complete restart.
